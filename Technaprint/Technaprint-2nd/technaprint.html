Services

Technaprint specializes in printing and decorating whether you are in the marketing, medical, business, sports, tools, bottles or electronics we are here for you to provide fast and affordable printing for all kinds of projects you may need, from single to full color printing.

We know that orders are often needed for events so we offer same-day services upon request as well as fulfillment services such as gift wrapping or special packaging so you can have peace of mind knowing that your project is just a phone call away.

Medical Device Printing

  • Biocompatible inks
  • Pretreatment
  • Catheter Tube Marking

Electronic Device Printing

  • Print on different shapes
  • Conductive inks
  • USB connectors
  • Keypads

Promotional Product Printing

  • Bottles and mugs
  • Flash drives and HDD's
  • Speakers & headphones
  • Power banks

Equipment

  • 10 pad print machines up to 4 color automatic
  • 2 cylinder screen printing systems
  • single color pad printing
  • product assembly, packaging and order fulfillment capabilities
